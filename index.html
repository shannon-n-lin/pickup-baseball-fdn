Free pickup baseball for all

Need more baseball in your life? Come out and play some ball at Pickup Baseball Foundation's weekly meetups! No registration, no fees, no pressure.

Join us in Oakland every Sunday at 12pm. Sign up below to get notified each week.

Whether you're new to the sport or have been playing for years, everyone is welcome.

About Pickup Baseball Foundation

Pickup Baseball Foundation (PBF) provides free access to pickup baseball for teens and adults of all ages, genders, and skill levels. We foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for building community with fellow baseball fans of all backgrounds in the greater San Francisco Bay Area. PBF is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded in 2021 and based in Oakland, CA.

Get in touch with us at pickupbaseballfoundation@gmail.com.
